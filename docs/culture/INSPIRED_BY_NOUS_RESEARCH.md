## Who Is Nous Research?

**Nous Research** is an open-source AI research collective turned company, founded in 2022 by **Jeffrey Quesnelle** (CEO), **Karan Malhotra** (Head of Behavior), **Teknium** (Head of Post-Training), and **Shivani Mitra** (Co-Founder). Based in New York City with a team of ~20 people, they've become one of the most significant forces in open-source AI.

**How they started:** A volunteer collective of AI researchers and developers who met on Discord, GitHub, and Twitter. They began by fine-tuning existing open-source models, creating the **Hermes** series — models that quickly became the most downloaded community-built LLMs on Hugging Face, with **over 33 million downloads**.

**How they grew:** A $5.2M seed round in 2024, followed by a **$50M Series A** led by Paradigm in April 2025, at a **$1 billion valuation**. But unlike most funded AI companies, they didn't close up. They doubled down on open source.

## Their Philosophy — And Why It's Ours Too

### "Artificial Intelligence Made Human"

This is Nous Research's tagline. It means AI should serve the user — not the corporation that built it. AI should make you a better, more capable version of yourself — not steer your attention for someone else's benefit.

**Sonic-Forage lives this.** Our autonomous DJ system has **closed gates** — nothing goes public without human approval. The AI works for the DJ, not the other way around. The human is always in control.

### Neutral Alignment — The User Decides

Nous Research believes AI models should be **"neutrally aligned"** — meaning the end user decides the values and personality of the model. Alignment is a tool for personal empowerment, not a hidden agenda imposed by a corporation.

**Sonic-Forage lives this.** Our DJ companion pets have different personalities. Our voice personas are customizable. Our set planning adapts to YOUR style, not some corporate template. The AI learns from YOU.

### Open Source as a Counterbalance

From their manifesto *"From Black Box to Glass House"*:

> "Open-source AI serves as a counterbalance to the otherwise unchecked power of private AI and the potential mass digital surveillance by corrupt corporations and governments. It ensures that no single entity holds the reins of great power and that no entity can hijack or veto technological progress."

**Sonic-Forage lives this.** Every repo is open source. Every file is forkable. No proprietary lock-in. No paywalls. No "premium features" hidden behind a subscription. If you can clone it, you can build it.

### Decentralization — Power to the People

Nous Research is building the **Psyche Network** — a decentralized AI training system that pools idle GPUs from around the world, coordinated on the Solana blockchain. Their **DisTrO optimizer** reduces inter-GPU communication by orders of magnitude, making globally distributed training feasible.

**Sonic-Forage lives this.** Our offline DJ pool works with no internet. Our system runs on any hardware — from a Raspberry Pi to a festival-grade server. The music doesn't stop when the WiFi goes down.

### Anti-Mode-Collapse — Let the Model Think

Nous Research explicitly opposes **"mode collapse"** — where AI models over-refuse legitimate questions. They created **RefusalBench** to measure how often models refuse to answer. Hermes 4 achieves a 60% non-refusal rate vs. ~5% for the open-source version of ChatGPT.

**Sonic-Forage lives this.** Our AI DJ doesn't refuse to play a genre. It doesn't have corporate guardrails that prevent it from mixing a hard techno track into a chill ambient set. It explores. It experiments. It learns.

### Education and Access

Nous Research believes **equal access and education around technologies result in safer development and awareness.** If only corporations have access to AI, the public can't understand or critique it. Widespread access creates widespread awareness.

**Sonic-Forage lives this.** Our rave culture field guide teaches 60+ years of history. Our DJ terminology guide has 200+ terms. Our how-to-DJ guide takes you from zero to your first set. Knowledge should be free.

## The Hermes Model Series

Nous Research's flagship product is the **Hermes** model series — open-source LLMs that have redefined what community-built AI can do:

| Model | Year | Based On | Key Achievement |
|-------|------|----------|----------------|
| **Hermes 1** | 2023 | Llama 13B | Ranked #1 on several benchmarks |
| **Hermes 2** | 2023 | Mixtral 8x7B DPO | Improved reasoning and conversation |
| **Hermes 3** | Aug 2024 | Llama 3.1 (8B/70B/405B) | 33M+ downloads, advanced roleplay, agentic function-calling |
| **Hermes 4** | Aug 2025 | Hybrid reasoning | 96.3% on MATH-500, 81.9% on AIME'24, 57.1% on RefusalBench |
| **Hermes 4.3** | 2026 | Psyche-trained | First model trained entirely on distributed network, 512K context |

**Hermes Agent** — the framework that powers Sonic-Forage — is how these models become persistent, self-improving agents with multi-platform support, persistent memory, and skill learning loops.

**Every line of Sonic-Forage was generated by Hermes Agent.** Zero human code. 390+ files. 14 repos. One AI, one night, one mission.

## Their Key Technologies

### DataForge
Graph-based synthetic data generator that creates training data through "random walks" through knowledge graphs. Powers 90%+ of Hermes training data.

### Atropos
Open-source reinforcement learning framework for aligning models with user preferences across 1,200+ tasks. Designed for thousands of distributed workers.

### Psyche Network
Coordinated on Solana blockchain — a decentralized AI training network that pools idle GPUs from around the world. In December 2024, they trained a 15B parameter model in a fully distributed manner.

### DisTrO (Distributed Training Over-the-Internet)
A breakthrough optimizer that reduces inter-GPU communication by orders of magnitude. The technical backbone of their decentralized vision.

### YaRN Method
Context-length extension method cited in 100+ academic papers, adopted by Meta and DeepSeek.

### DeMo Paper
Collaborative research with **Diederik P. Kingma** (OpenAI co-founder, co-inventor of the Adam optimizer) on decentralized optimization.
