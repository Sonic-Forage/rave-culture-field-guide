# Project Guide: What We Are Doing Here

This repo is a culture map and launch artifact. It is meant to help a newcomer understand rave history while also giving builders/DJs/community organizers a practical guide.

## Goals

- Tell the story from underground roots to now.
- Explain why the RAVE Act and anti-rave policy matter.
- Keep harm reduction and consent central.
- Teach enough DJ language to help someone start practicing.
- Use a generated hero image as a visual story anchor.
- Stay fun, PLUR-coded, and honest about complexity.

## Non-goals

- Not legal advice.
- Not medical advice.
- Not a drug-use manual.
- Not a claim that one city, race, scene, or genre “owns” the entire story.
- Not a replacement for learning from elders, locals, working DJs, organizers, and harm-reduction professionals.
